About the Project

The Deconstructionist Cookbook examines the intersection of food, algorithms, and cultural narratives. By combining computational analysis with personal food memories, we aim to reveal how our understanding of cuisine is shaped by both technology and lived experience.

This project critically examines how algorithms classify and categorize our food choices, while also celebrating the personal stories and memories that give meaning to our culinary experiences.
